# NIST CSF 2.0
_Stands for **N**ational **I**nstitute of **S**tandards and **T**echnology **C**yber**s**ecurity **F**ramework version 2._

_This work is cited from NIST CSF website which is available online at [www.nist.gov/cyberframework](https://www.nist.gov/cyberframework)._

### NIST CSF Profiles
_It `describes current and/or target cybersecurity posture in an organization` in terms of NIST CSF Core's outcomes._

_CSF Organizational Profiles are classified into two subject areas:_
1. CSF 2.0 Organizational Profiles
2. CSF 2.0 Community Profiles

_**Purposes** of Organizational Profiles within an organization are:_
- to understand, tailor, assess, prioritize, communicate the CSF Core's outcomes.

_**Main considerations** of Organizational Profiles are:_
- mission objectives
- stakeholder expectations
- threat landscape
- requirements

_ Each Organizational Profile includes one or both of the following:_
1. Current Profile
2. Target Profile
